# Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard designed to analyze listing trends, market share, pricing, and customer ratings across major cities.
#### The dashboard helps stakeholders understand:
- Growth in Airbnb listings over time
- Market dominance of top cities
- Pricing patterns across property types
- Customer satisfaction metrics
## Dashboard Preview
- Overview Dashboard
- Ratings Dashboard (Bar View)
- Ratings Dashboard (Detailed Heatmap)
## Key Insights
### Listings Growth
- Airbnb experienced rapid growth, peaking in 2015.
- Growth slowed in 2016–2017 due to regulatory changes.
- A decline was observed in 2019–2020, mainly due to COVID-19.
### Market Share by City
- Paris, New York, and Sydney contribute nearly 50% of total listings and reviews.
- Paris leads in both listings and reviews.
### Pricing Insights
- Hotel rooms are the most expensive (~$800).
- Entire homes/apartments (~$673) are the most popular.
- Private rooms are the most affordable (~$462).
### Ratings Analysis
- Top-rated cities: Mexico City, Rio de Janeiro
- Lowest-rated cities: Hong Kong, Istanbul
#### Key weak areas:
- Cleanliness
- Value for money
